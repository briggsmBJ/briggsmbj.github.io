How to Retrieve a BitLocker Recovery Key from Intune

  1. Sign in to the Microsoft Intune admin center.
  2. Navigate to Devices > All devices.
  3. Select the device for which you want to retrieve the BitLocker recovery key.
  4. Under Monitor, click on Recovery keys.
  5. The BitLocker recovery key will be displayed. You can copy or export it as needed.
